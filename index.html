Team

Massa team is comstrongosed of 30+ developers (specialized in Blockchain Technology & Rust), based in 5 countries.

What started as a research project between three friends in 2017 became a reality in 2020 when Sébastien Forestier, Damir Vodenicarevic and Adrien Laversanne-Finot registered Massa Labs as a company. The Massa team has been growing fast, with now 35 professionals. 30 of them are focused on the development of the Massa software, including the smart contract engine, and 4 of them are dedicated to communication, marketing, partnerships, legal and operations. You can join us.
